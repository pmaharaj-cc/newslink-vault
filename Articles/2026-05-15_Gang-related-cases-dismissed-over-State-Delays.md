## Background

Garvey Campo, 38, was shot dead at a house in Windy Hill, Arouca, in January 2019. Police recovered spent shells marked 'TTPS' at the scene, prompting a Police Complaints Authority investigation into how police-issued ammunition ended up at the murder scene.
